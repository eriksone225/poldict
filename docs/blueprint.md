# **App Name**: Poldict

## Core Features:

- Account Creation & Role Management: Allow users to create accounts and assign roles (admin/player).
- Votable Creation: Players and admins can create votable items with descriptions and defined outcomes.
- Votable Deletion: Admins can delete any votable; players can only delete their own.
- Voting System: Enable users to vote on votable items.
- Outcome Determination: Allow admins to mark votables as 'happened' or 'not happened'.
- Points Distribution: Automatically award points to users who correctly predicted the outcome of a votable.
- Leaderboard: Display a leaderboard of users ranked by their total points.

## Style Guidelines:

- Banner: #0F0C45
- Banner Text: White
- Background: White
- Votable Buttons: Green and Red
- Bolded letters or headings: #0F0C45
- Texts: #9B9B9B
- Font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, for both headlines and body text.
- Use minimalist icons to represent different categories of votable items and user actions.
- Prioritize clear data presentation for the leaderboard and voting options.
- Subtle transitions for voting confirmations and leaderboard updates.
